Our History

A Legacy in Synthesis

Established in the year 2017, the Department of Chemistry at Pandit Deendayal Upadhyaya Adarsha Mahavidyalaya (PDUAM), Dalgaon, was founded with a vision to bridge the gap between rural academic talent and high-level chemical research. Since its inception, the department has transitioned from a foundational undergraduate wing to a sophisticated center for scientific inquiry in the Darrang district.

Our journey began with a single multipurpose laboratory, which has now evolved into a specialized multi-wing facility housing Advanced Synthesis, Instrumental Analysis, and Digital Simulation units. We take pride in our alumni who have successfully secured positions in premier institutes like IITs, NITs, and Central Universities.

"Our mission is to translate theoretical molecular concepts into tangible industrial solutions while nurturing a research-first mindset in every student."
