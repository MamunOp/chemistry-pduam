A Legacy of
Scientific Rigor

Established in 2017, the Department of Chemistry has been the cornerstone of science education at PDUAM Dalgaon. Our history is one of constant evolution—from a single laboratory setup to a multi-wing research center.

Our Vision

To be a globally recognized center of chemical excellence that empowers local talent to solve global environmental and industrial challenges.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality undergraduate research facilities and bridge the gap between rural education and modern scientific advancement.
