Legacy

History of the Department

The Department of Chemistry at PDUAM Dalgaon began its journey in 2017 with a vision to provide quality science education in the Darrang district. Over the years, it has grown into a hub of academic activity, producing graduates who excel in both higher education and industrial applications.

Vision

To be a premier center of chemical education and research that empowers students with knowledge and skills to address global challenges.

Mission Statements

  • To provide a strong foundation in theoretical and practical aspects of chemical sciences.
  • To cultivate a culture of research and curiosity among undergraduate students.
  • To bridge the gap between academic learning and industrial requirements through practical exposure.
