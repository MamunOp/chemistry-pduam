The Chronicle

A Legacy of Scientific Inquiry

Established in 2017, the Department of Chemistry at PDUAM Dalgaon was founded with a transformative vision: to bring high-caliber science education to the rural landscapes of Darrang. What began as a foundational undergraduate wing has evolved into a powerhouse of chemical education, bridging the gap between local talent and global scientific standards.

Our history is marked by a relentless pursuit of infrastructure excellence. Over the last seven years, we have transitioned from basic experimental setups to a multi-wing facility that supports Advanced Synthesis and Instrumental Analysis. Our faculty, composed of researchers from premier national institutes, ensures that the spirit of 'Synthesis and Discovery' remains at the core of our pedagogy.

Our Vision

To be a pioneer in chemical education that nurtures a research-centric mindset, enabling students to address global environmental and industrial challenges.

Our Mission

Providing state-of-the-art laboratory access and 100% mentorship for graduates to excel in prestigious national-level research entrances (JAM, GATE).
