Our History

A Legacy of Scientific Excellence

Founded in 2017, the Department of Chemistry at PDUAM Dalgaon began with a singular mission: to democratize world-class science education in rural Assam. Over the past seven years, it has transformed from a foundational academic wing into a premier center for chemical research and analytical inquiry.

We take pride in our "Research-First" methodology, ensuring that every student moves beyond textbooks into real-world synthesis and molecular analysis. Our alumni now represent us in top national laboratories and central universities across India.

Our Vision

To be a pioneer in sustainable chemical education that produces innovative thinkers capable of solving industrial and environmental crises.

Our Mission

Empowering rural talent through 100% laboratory access, digital ICT learning tools, and rigorous competitive exam mentorship (JAM/GATE).
